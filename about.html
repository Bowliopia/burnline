About Burnline

Burnline is a digital magazine that lives at the luminous edge of the fractured future.

We publish stories, art, and commentary that reflect the beauty, despair, and thrill of cyberpunk worlds—whether grimy and neon-lit, synthetic and spiritual, or hyperreal and collapsing in on themselves.

We're here for the creators. The dreamers. The ones writing code and poetry in the same breath. Our mission is to give voice to the untold stories of tomorrow and to elevate the creators pushing boundaries in genre fiction and visual expression.

If you’d like to submit, collaborate, or just say hi, head over to our Submit page or reach out at contact@burnline.net.
