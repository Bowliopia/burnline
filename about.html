About Burnline

Burnline is a digital magazine run by a small crew who's tried our best to make a hub for all things cyberpunk.

We publish stories, art, and commentary that reflects the weirdness of whatever the hell cyberpunk is supposed to be. Feel like the real world is folding in on itself? Us too. Let's make some art about it.

If you’d like to submit, collaborate, or just say hi, head over to our Submit page or reach out at contact@burnline.net. If there's anything we don't express on this site that you think belongs here, we'd love to hear about it.

Sign up to our newsletter to get the latest stories, art, and neon-lit news from Burnline.
